# Game of aliens 


## 🛸 Alien Guess the race

Step into the galaxy’s greatest guessing game! In Alien Name Challenge, you’re faced with mysterious cards hiding the names of strange and exotic alien races. Your mission? Use your wit and deduction skills to correctly guess the alien race hidden behind each card.

Every correct guess brings you closer to ultimate bragging rights and if you manage to guess 5 alien names in a row, you’ll unlock the opportunity to acquire an exclusive Alien NFT 🪐.

Think you’ve got what it takes to outsmart interstellar mysteries and claim your prize? The universe is waiting!
